Privacy Policy — ReflexRealm

Effective date: September 18, 2025

Overview

ReflexRealm does not collect, store, or share personal data. All user-generated content and settings remain on the device.

Network Use

The app does not require an account and does not transmit personal information to MadeInDK servers. If the app checks for updates or loads remote content (e.g., help pages or legal text), these requests do not include personal identifiers.

Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.

Permissions

If ReflexRealm requests optional permissions (e.g., Photos or Camera), they are used only for the corresponding on-device feature. Content stays on the device unless the user explicitly shares it.

Security

Local data is stored using iOS standard application storage. Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored data according to iOS behavior.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact: factionui@gmail.com
