Privacy Policy — ReflexRealm

Effective date: September 28, 2025

By Thomas Enevoldsen and/or XBuildStudio

Overview

ReflexRealm does not collect, store, or share personal data with us directly. Game progress and settings remain on the user’s device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may automatically collect and process certain information, such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers — IDFA, if permission is granted)
  • Approximate location data
  • App usage information (e.g. screens viewed, interactions with ads)
  • Diagnostic and performance data to improve ad delivery

This data is used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, to limit repetitive ads, and to measure ad effectiveness. Users can control whether personalized ads are allowed via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt and device privacy settings.

You can review Google’s AdMob privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Permissions

If ReflexRealm requests optional permissions (e.g., Photos or Camera), they are used only for the corresponding on-device feature. Content stays on the device unless the user explicitly shares it.

Security

Local data is stored using iOS standard application storage. Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored data according to iOS behavior.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal data through ads, please contact us.
