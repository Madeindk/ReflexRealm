Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 28, 2025

By Thomas Enevoldsen and/or XBuildStudio

This policy applies to all apps developed and published by MadeInDK, unless an app-specific policy states otherwise.

Data Collection

Our apps themselves do not collect, store, or share personal information with us directly. App data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to our own servers.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Some of our apps use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process certain data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers — IDFA, if permission is granted)
  • Approximate location data
  • App usage information (e.g. which screens are viewed, interactions with ads)
  • Diagnostic and performance data to improve ad delivery

This data is used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, to limit repetitive ads, and to measure ad effectiveness. Users can control whether personalized ads are allowed via the system’s App Tracking Transparency prompt and iOS privacy settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal data through ads, please contact us and we will work with our partners to address it.

Third-Party Privacy Policy

You can review Google’s AdMob privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
