Privacy Policy — MadeInDK Apps

Effective date: September 28, 2025

By Thomas Enevoldsen and/or XBuildStudio

This policy applies to all apps developed and published by MadeInDK, unless an app-specific policy states otherwise.

Data Collection

Our apps themselves do not collect, store, or share personal information with us directly. App data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to our own servers.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Most of our apps use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process certain data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers — IDFA, if permission is granted)
  • Approximate location data
  • App usage information (e.g. which screens are viewed, interactions with ads)
  • Diagnostic and performance data to improve ad delivery

This data is used for personalized or non-personalized advertising, limiting repetitive ads, and measuring ad effectiveness. Users control personalized ads via iOS App Tracking Transparency and system privacy settings.

Google’s AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided data through ads, please contact us.
